Electric Vehicle Solar Charging System

Electric Vehicle Solar Charging System is a a project that combines the capabilities of solar energy, computation and networking. The project makes use of solar panels to generate electricity and a microcontroller system (arduino) to allocate charging time for users electric vehicles. Once the users have booked a charging slot that is dependant on the class of their vehicle they can proceed to activate the slot and charge the vehicle during the allocated time.

The system provides the facilities to charge the following make of electric vehicles.

  1. Electric Cars
  2. E-Bike
  3. Tricycle (Tuck Tuck)

To schedule a charging slot for your make of vehicle please use the booking service below. An Email and SMS confirmation will be sent to your device for confirmation
